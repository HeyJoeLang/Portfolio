Apollo is a top down stealth game with a heavy
influence from the Metal Gear series. There are
guards and cameras to avoid, keys to collect,
a mini-game when the player is caught, and a 
boss battle at the end of the game.

Controls:

While Sneaking:
Directional keys: move

While in caught mini-game:
Mouse: move

While in boss fight:
Directional keys: move
clicking drop mine button: drops mine
